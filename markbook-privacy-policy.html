Last Updated: 28.11.2024

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app. Baby Markbook collects and stores your personal information and data when you (“user”) use application.

Types of Information and Method of Collecting

Personal Information

Baby Markbook application make use of third party SDKs that serve to provide us and the user with advertisement, and analytics of user behavior. These third party services may collect and use various personal information including, but not limited to, geolocation, advertisement identifiers and IP address.

Non-Personal Information

The same third party services as mentioned above may also collect and use non-personal information related to the user such as device type, OS, screen resolution, language and geolocation.

Security

We follow accepted industry standards and maintain reasonable safeguards to protect the information submitted to us.

Use of Data

The information collected by AdMob is used to provide personalized ads that are more relevant to you. This helps us to improve your experience with our app by showing ads that are tailored to your interests.
