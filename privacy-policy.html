Pocket Tools Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 24.10.2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app.

Data Collection

We use Google AdMob to serve ads in our app. AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including but not limited to:

Device information (e.g., device type, operating system, unique device identifiers) App usage data (e.g., the frequency and duration of app usage) Location data (if location services are enabled on your device)

Use of Data

The information collected by AdMob is used to provide personalized ads that are more relevant to you. This helps us to improve your experience with our app by showing ads that are tailored to your interests.

Third-Party Sharing

The data collected by AdMob may be shared with third-party advertising partners to deliver personalized ads. We do not share your personal information with any other third parties without your explicit consent.
